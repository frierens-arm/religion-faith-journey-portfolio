Wisdom
Having knowledge and actually knowing what to do with it.
Wisdom — What It Means
Scripture & Faith
Definition
Wisdom is having knowledge and actually knowing what to do with it. It is making good choices and being honest when you mess up.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine is wise because he admitted he was wrong. He spent half his life chasing useless things like fame and pleasure. He admitted his mistakes after.
Old Testament — Solomon
Solomon did not ask God for money or power. He asked for wisdom to lead God's people. He could have asked for anything but he chose wisdom.
Wisdom — My Experience
Real Life
What I Did
I got into an argument with my parents and I knew it was my fault. I just admitted it instead of keep going.
Real Life Example
My table tennis coach shows me things that applies to normal life, not just ping pong. One thing he taught me. Never look at someone and judge them on one small thing. Look at the full picture. That is basically what wisdom is.
Pride
Pride is when people only look at someone's weak points. Pride makes you think you already know everything. That is the opposite of being wise.
Wisdom — Self Reflection
Reflection
When It Was Easy
It was easy to show wisdom when nobody was watching. Just me and my parents.
When It Was Hard
It is harder when people are around and you feel like you have to save face.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is think before I speak. A lot of times I say things without thinking. If I just paused for one second, I would not get into half the arguments I get into.
Wisdom — Prayer
Creative Expression
Lord, help me admit when I am wrong.
Even when it is embarrassing.
Teach me to see the full picture,
not just the parts that are easy to judge.
Amen.
March
Perseverance
Not giving up. Even when things are hard. Even when you are frustrated.
Perseverance — What the Virtue Means
Scripture & Faith
Definition
Perseverance is not giving up. Even when things are hard. Even when you are frustrated. You just keep going. That is it. There is no secret trick. You just do not stop.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine lived a bad life for a long time. But he never stopped looking for God. He kept searching through all the wrong ways until he found the right one.
Old Testament — Moses
Moses was not a good speaker and Pharaoh kept saying no. Over and over. But Moses just kept going back. He did not give up on what God asked him to do.
"Let us not become weary in doing good, for at the proper time we will reap a harvest if we do not give up." — Galatians 6:9
Perseverance — My Experience
Real Life
What I Did
Year 3 was the worst I have played table tennis in five years. But I did not stop. I kept training. It was frustrating but quitting would have been worse. And when I teach at camps, my patience gets tested a lot. Nothing is working and nobody is improving. But you just keep going.
Real Life Example
Think about players like Ma Long or Miwa Harimoto. If they quit when they were down 0-3, they would never have come back to win 4-3 and become champions. They just never stop.
Sloth
Sloth is a real problem. People quit when they do not see results right away. But that is kind of the whole point. You keep going even when nothing is happening.
Perseverance — Self Reflection
Reflection
When It Was Easy
It was easy to persevere when I was getting better. When I could see progress, it kept me going.
When It Was Hard
It is harder when I feel stuck.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is set smaller goals. Instead of thinking about the big picture every day, just focus on getting one thing better. Then it does not feel so overwhelming.
Perseverance — Prayer
Creative Expression
Lord, I want to quit sometimes.
A lot of times actually.

Help me remember why I started.
Even when nothing is working.
Even when nothing is changing.

I am going to keep going.
Amen.
April
Reverence
More than just being polite. Actually respecting someone. Treating them like they matter.
Reverence — What the Virtue Means
Scripture & Faith
Definition
Reverence is more than just being polite. It is actually respecting someone. Treating them like they matter. Because they do.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine eventually changed his entire life because he started respecting God. He did not just go through the motions. He actually changed.
Old Testament — King David
David had the chance to kill Saul. Saul was trying to kill him first. But David spared him because Saul was chosen by God. He respected that even when it did not make sense.
"Show proper respect to everyone." — 1 Peter 2:17
Reverence — My Experience
Real Life
What I Did
I showed respect to my parents and my table tennis coach. It is easy to respect someone who helps you. But my coach gives advice to everyone at the club. Even people who do not like him. I respect that a lot.
Real Life Example
I respect my dad a lot. He can talk to anyone. Anywhere. Strangers, family, does not matter. He treats everyone the same. And I want to be like that.
Envy
Envy is when people cannot respect others because they are jealous. If people just appreciated what they have instead of being jealous, things would be a lot better.
Reverence — Self Reflection
Reflection
When It Was Easy
It was easy to respect my parents and coach because they have been there for me.
When It Was Hard
It is harder respecting people who are rude to you first. My natural reaction is to be rude back.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is remember everyone is fighting their own battles. Somebody being mean might have nothing to do with me. If I treat them with respect anyway, that means more.
Reverence — Prayer
Creative Expression
Lord, help me treat people with respect.
Not just the people I like.
Everyone.

When I feel jealous, remind me
what I already have.
Amen.
May
Responsibility
Owning what you do. Not blaming other people. Not making excuses.
Responsibility — What the Virtue Means
Scripture & Faith
Definition
Responsibility means owning what you do. Not blaming other people. Not making excuses.
St. Augustine
After St. Augustine found God, he did not just say sorry and move on. He became a bishop and spent the rest of his life helping people. He took responsibility for his past and did something about it.
Old Testament — Noah
God told Noah to build an ark. He did not complain. He did not make excuses. He just did it and saved his family and the animals.
Responsibility — My Experience
Real Life
What I Did
I helped my partners finish our science project. It is easy to be responsible when you feel good. It is harder when you are exhausted and just want to sleep but you know you have something to finish.
Real Life Example
My dad goes to work every day. Even when he is tired. He shows up. That is responsibility and most people do not even think about it.
Greed
Greed is when people only care about themselves. If everyone just did their own part, things would actually work.
Responsibility — Self Reflection
Reflection
When It Was Easy
It was easy to be responsible when I had energy and things were going fine with the project.
When It Was Hard
It is harder when I am tired and I have something due the next day but I just want to give up.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is stop procrastinating. My lack of responsibility comes from waiting until the last minute. If I started earlier, I would not be so stressed.
Responsibility — Prayer
Creative Expression
Lord, help me be someone people can count on.
When I am tired, give me strength.
Help me own what I do.
Not just the easy stuff.
The hard stuff too.
Amen.
Closing

Growing in faith.
One virtue at a time.

Lord, thank you for these virtues.
Help me use them even when no one is watching.

I am not going to be perfect.
But I am going to keep trying.
Amen.
