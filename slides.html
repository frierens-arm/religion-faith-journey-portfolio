February
Wisdom
Wisdom — What It Means
Definition
Wisdom is having knowledge and actually knowing what to do with it. It is making good choices and being honest when you mess up.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine is wise because he admitted he was wrong. He spent half his life chasing useless things like fame and pleasure. He admitted his mistakes after.
Old Testament — Solomon
Solomon did not ask God for money or power. He asked for wisdom to lead God's people. He could have asked for anything but he chose wisdom.
Wisdom — My Experience
What I Did
I got into an argument with my parents and I knew it was my fault. I just admitted it instead of keep going.
Real Life
My table tennis coach shows me things that applies to normal life, not just ping pong. One thing he taught me. Never look at someone and judge them on one small thing. Look at the full picture. That is basically what wisdom is.
Pride
Pride is when people only look at someone's weak points. Pride makes you think you already know everything. That is the opposite of being wise.
Wisdom — Prayer
Lord, help me admit when I am wrong.
Even when it is embarrassing.
Guide me to see the full picture,
not just the easy parts.
Amen.
March
Perseverance
Perseverance — What the Virtue Means
Definition
Perseverance is not giving up even when things are hard or even when you are frustrated.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine lived a bad life for a long time. But he never stopped looking for God. He kept searching through all the wrong ways until he found the right one.
Old Testament — Moses
When Moses was talking to the Pharaoh about freeing the Hebrew people from slavery, the Pharaoh kept saying no over and over again. But Moses just kept going back and kept persevering on what God asked him to do.
Perseverance — My Experience
What I Did
In table tennis, my worst year of playing was the 3rd year out of 5 years of playing. During the 3rd year I did not stop and kept on training. When I teach at summer camps, my patience gets tested a lot but you just keep going and training to improve and i feel like this is a good example of perseverance.
Real Life
Think about my favourite table tennis players like Ma Long or Miwa Harimoto. If they quit when they were down 0-3, they would never have come back to win 4-3 and become champions.
Sloth
People quit when they do not see results right away but that is kind of the whole point to improve. You keep going even when you do not feel like you are improving.
Perseverance — Self Reflection
It was easy to persevere when I am physically getting better. When I could see progress, it would fuel me to train harder. It is harder when I feel stuck but in a game, you either win or lose and I cannot win every game.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is set smaller goals. Instead of thinking about the big picture every day, just focus on getting one thing better.
April
Reverence
Reverence — What the Virtue Means
Definition
Reverence is actually respecting someone. Treating them like they matter.
St. Augustine
St. Augustine eventually changed his entire life because he started having reverence for God.
Old Testament — King David
David had the chance to kill Saul even if Saul was trying to kill him first. But David spared him because Saul was chosen by God and he had reverence for God's decision.
Reverence — My Experience
What I Did
I showed respect to my table tennis coach. It is easy to have reverence for someone who helps you. But my coach gives advice to everyone at the club, even to people who do not like him.
Real Life
I respect my dad a lot. He can talk to anyone. Anywhere. Strangers, family, does not matter. He treats everyone the same. And I want to be like that.
Envy
Envy is when people do not have reverence for others because they are jealous. People should just appreciated what they have.
Reverence — Prayer
Lord, help me treat people with reverence.
Not just the people I know.

When I feel jealous,
Remind me what I already have.
Amen.
May
Responsibility
Responsibility — What the Virtue Means
Definition
Responsibility means owning what you do instead of blaming other people to making excuses.
St. Augustine
After St. Augustine found God, He became a bishop and spent the rest of his life helping people. He took responsibility.
Old Testament — Noah
God told Noah to build an ark. He did not complain. He did not make excuses. He just did it and saved his family and the animals.

God told Noah to build and ark. Noah built an ark for his family and 2 sets of every animal instead of making an excuse on how he cannot do it, which shows he has responsability.
Responsibility — My Experience
What I Did
I helped my partners finish our science project. It is easy to be responsible when you are full of energy so when everybody else puts of on the work, I can just do it.
Real Life
My Father goes to work every day. Even when he is tired or hurt he never takes break days. That is responsibility and I hope I can carry this on when im older.
Greed
Greed is when people only care about themselves. If everyone just did their own part, things would actually work.
Responsibility — Self Reflection
It was easy to be responsible when I have energy at the beginning of the school year. It is harder when I am tired or I have something due the next day.
How I Can Improve
One thing I can do better is to stop procrastinating. I have little responsibility because I wait until a few dayhs before a due date to finish working on a project. I would not be so stressed if I just had some responsibility to do my projects and homework.
